Privacy Directive.

Effective Date: October 2024

Your privacy is integral. This directive outlines our methodology for data handling within the Pixora ecosystem.

1. Data Collection Architecture

We utilize minimal data footprints. Only essential authentication parameters (e.g., email vectors) and curated preferences (e.g., saved artifacts) are persisted over secure local or server instances.

2. Interaction Tracking

Our visual archives dynamically read your cursor topology to execute the magnetic and tracking animations globally. These metrics occur entirely client-side and are immediately purged after frame rendering.

3. External Syndication

We do not transfer, syndicate, or broker your interaction data to unverified third parties. Art consumption is personal, and our architecture respects that boundary.
